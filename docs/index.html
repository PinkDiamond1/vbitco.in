Virtual Bitcoin is Bitcoin implemented as a smart contract

By inheriting the strengths and characteristics of Bitcoin, it can be mined and used as a store of value. It is completely decentralized.

Characteristics from Bitcoin

  • Maximum of 21 million
  • Mining
  • Halving

Characteristics from ERC-20

  • Available in smart contracts
  • No additional power consumption
